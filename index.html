About

Hi, I'm Himanshu — a digital marketer who got tired of handing designs off to someone else, so I learned to build the thing myself.

I finished my B.Sc at Kumaun University and moved straight into digital marketing, starting with social media, SEO and Canva creatives at Digital Quest. Since then I've picked up the technical side too — I now work directly with IT teams on WordPress and .NET-based websites, using JavaScript, MongoDB, Supabase and GitHub to take projects from campaign brief to live, working site.

What I like most is the seam between the two disciplines: writing the keyword strategy in the morning and shipping the page it lives on by the afternoon. Currently doing exactly that as a Digital Marketer at Herbal Creations, Haldwani.
